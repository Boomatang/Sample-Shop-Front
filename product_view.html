User Support

  1. The four levels of support are.

    • On-line help is the lowest level of help. This is where the user finds their own help. It comes from good help documentation in the software and turning to the Internet forums to find information and help. It is the user going out and finding other user who may have had the same problem or knows how to solve the problems.

      It is possible that a user will gain the best long term information this way, as nothing will be handed to them. The user needs to learn to solve the problems themselves.

      For this to work successfully there needs to be large groups using the software or hardware in question. It will not work well for bespoke set ups.

    • Front line support. The user is meet by a support personnel that is wanting to promote the system in question. These support personnel may not have a technical understanding of the problems and provide quick-fix solutions as well as non-technical support.

      It would be quite possible that the front line personnel would be reading from a list of predetermined answer to the problems. That is by no means saying that its not the right answer. Just if the user problem goes outside the scope of the answer, then the problem may be passed on to the next stage.

    • The third level of support is the second line support. The support that is provide at this level is more technical. Support here will delve more in to the workings of the system over the user interaction with the system. To get to this level of support a user will first have to pass through the front line support.

      The support personnel here will be very technical and can be based in house or by an external company. They will have great knowledge of the system and can solve the majority of problems. Not many problems will pass this level of support and any that kjbido will be major problems.

    • For the major problems there is the third line. This is the last line of support if a problem reach this level there is some real issues. The system builders, analysts and designers make up this support. Costs of this support will be taken up in a maintenance contract or on an issue by issue bases.

      Taken down or even rebuilding sections of a system are a real possibility at this stage. End results of a new system been need to be develop could come out as been the easiest and cheapest fix to a problem.
